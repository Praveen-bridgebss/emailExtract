Most Gmail accounts require an App Password for IMAP access. Your regular password will likely fail.

Step 1: Enable IMAP in Gmail
  1. Go to Gmail Settings
  2. Click "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab
  3. Enable "IMAP access"
  4. Click "Save Changes"
Step 2: Generate App Password
  1. Go to Google Security Settings
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification (if not already enabled)
  3. Go to "App passwords" section
  4. Select "Mail" and "Other (custom name)"
  5. Enter "Email Parser App" as the name
  6. Copy the 16-character password (no spaces)
